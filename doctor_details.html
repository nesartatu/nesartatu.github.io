Dr. Lamiya Chowdhury

Orthodontist
BDS (Dhaka), PhD in Orthodontics (Japan)
Fellow WFO

Dr. Lamiya Chowdhury graduated from Dhaka Dental College under the University of Dhaka, Bangladesh and obtained her PhD degree in Orthodontics from Hokkaido University School of dentistry, Sapporo, Japan. She received her clinical training from Hokkaido University Dental Hospital. Dr. Lamiya attended many national and international seminars & symposium. She is a fellow of WFO (World Federation of Orthodontists). She also obtained diploma in Food and Nutrition Management from Centennial College, Toronto, Canada. She worked as a Supervisor nutrition department at UHN (University Health Network, a healthcare and research organization) which is affiliated with the University of Toronto. Dr. Lamiya started her teaching career as Assistant Professor in the department of Orthodontics, Bangladesh Dental College. Later she worked as Associate Professor and head of the department in the department of Orthodontics at Sapporo Dental College, Dhaka, Bangladesh. She is a founder member of FHESR (The Foundation for Health Education Service & Research), Dhaka, Bangladesh
