Care Plus Braces & Dental Care was started with the dedication towards providing quality dental care that you and your family deserve. Dr. Md. Abul Hassan Mahmud

Periodontology
BDS (Dhaka), PhD in Periodontology, DD (Canada)
Periodontologist & Endodontologist
Advanced Training on Oral Implants and Dental Prosthesis

Dr. Md. Abul Hassan Mahmud graduated from Dhaka Dental College, University of Dhaka, Bangladesh and received his PhD degree in Dental Science (Periodontology & Endodontology) from Hokkaido University School of Dentistry, Sapporo, Japan. He obtained his clinical training from the same university hospital and attended many clinical courses & symposiums. Dr. Hassan also obtained advanced diploma in dental prosthesis from George Brown College centre for health science, Toronto, Canada. He was a faculty member of George Brown College centre for health science and he worked in the WAVE dental clinic in this institution. Dr. Mahmud joined as Assistant Professor in the department of Endodontics, Bangladesh Dental College and later continued as Associate Professor and head of the department in the department of Endodontics at Sapporo Dental College. He is a founder member of FHESR (The Foundation for Health Education Service & Research).
